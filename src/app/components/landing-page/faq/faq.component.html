How many Instagram accounts I can add to my AgoraMonitor account?

You can add up to 6 different accounts.

Can I change my default account?

Yes, you can, from the ‘Manage Accounts’ tab you will be able to set any of your accounts as default.

What Instagram content I can search on AgoraMonitor?

Currently, you can search Instagram users and hashtags. Soon you will be able to search Instagram posts in specific location.
